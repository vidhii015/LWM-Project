About us

London Wealth Management is here to help grow and protect your wealth.

London Wealth Management was founded as an alternative to the big wealth management companies, with comparable levels of expertise but with a more personal experience. We are a financial planning-led investment management firm with a professional and personal approach.

We have built a team of professionally qualified specialists with the experience and expertise to give you the very highest level of service. When you work with us you benefit from the collective knowledge of our team, each dedicated to supporting you and striving to deliver the best possible financial outcome.

Our purpose is to give you the peace-of-mind that the advice you are receiving is delivered by highly qualified professionals who understand your true financial goals and objectives.

London Wealth Management’s history

London Wealth Management was founded by Paul Hoban, Ben Howland and Edward Tudor each of whom have worked together at larger financial advisory firms for a decade prior to founding LWM. Our mission was to create a better way to deliver a service that combines financial planning and investment management. London Wealth Management was established with serving clients interests at the heart of everything we do, with the belief that every client deserves and needs a personal service.

London Wealth Management was established in 2008 during what has now become known as the Global Financial Crisis. London Wealth Management has subsequently been through several global and economic shocks such as the Euro crisis, Brexit, the US election, the COVID-19 pandemic, the Russian invasion of Ukraine and the cost of living crisis.

Our Financial Planning led Investment Management service has proven robust in the most volatile periods of modern times. We pride ourselves on having a strong corporate culture and are always updating our work processes, continually improving and evolving with changing regulation and client needs.
